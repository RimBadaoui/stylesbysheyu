Salon Policies

Important guidelines for a smooth and professional experience

Hair Preparation Requirements

Come with Your Hair Washed and Blow Dried

To ensure the best results and maintain our schedule, please arrive with clean, washed, and blow-dried hair. This allows us to begin your service immediately and achieve optimal styling results.

Exception: Unless you have been specifically told otherwise during your consultation or booking confirmation.

  • Use a clarifying shampoo to remove all product buildup
  • Thoroughly condition your hair for manageability
  • Blow dry your hair completely before arrival
  • Avoid applying heavy oils or styling products before your appointment

Punctuality & Late Fees

Timeliness is Essential

We value both your time and our commitment to all clients. To maintain our schedule and provide quality service to everyone:

Late Fee Policy

An extra charge will be applied if you are 15+ minutes late with no contact. Please call or text if you're running behind schedule.

  • Arrive 5-10 minutes early to allow for check-in
  • Contact us immediately if you're running late
  • Late fees help compensate for schedule disruptions
  • Excessive lateness may result in rescheduling

Braiding Hair & Supplies

Bring Your Own Hair

Braiding hair will not be provided unless arranged for special circumstances during booking. This ensures you get exactly the color, texture, and quality you prefer.

  • Purchase high-quality braiding hair from trusted suppliers
  • Bring 20-30% more hair than estimated for your style
  • Pre-stretched hair is preferred for easier installation
  • Contact us for hair type and amount recommendations

Special Circumstances: If you need assistance with hair selection or emergency supply, please discuss this during booking. Additional fees may apply.

No Call, No Show Policy

Communication is Key

Strict Cancellation Policy

No contact after 30+ minutes will result in cancellation and potential ban for future appointments.

We understand that emergencies happen, but consistent communication helps us serve all our clients better.

  • Call or text if you need to cancel or reschedule
  • Provide at least 24-hour notice when possible
  • Emergency situations are handled case-by-case
  • Repeated no-shows may result in booking restrictions

Payment & General Policies

Service Investment

Quality hair artistry is an investment in your beauty and hair health. We accept various payment methods for your convenience.

  • Cash, card, and digital payments accepted
  • Deposits may be required for extensive services
  • Pricing discussed and confirmed before service begins
  • Touch-up policies vary by service type

Professional Environment

We maintain a professional, respectful salon environment for all clients and staff.

  • Respectful communication is expected
  • Children must be supervised at all times
  • Food and drinks should be discussed beforehand
  • Phone calls should be kept to a minimum during service

Questions About Our Policies?

We're here to help! If you have any questions about our policies or need clarification before your appointment, please don't hesitate to reach out.
